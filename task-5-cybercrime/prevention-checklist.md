# Stay Safe Online
**Prevention Tips:**

* Use strong, unique passwords for every account and enable two-factor authentication.
* Never share OTPs, passwords, or banking details with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank.
* Verify UPI requests carefully before approving; always check the recipient name and amount.
* Do not scan random QR codes or accept unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps.
* Avoid clicking on suspicious links sent via email, SMS, or social media.
* Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
* Limit the personal information you share on social media platforms.
* Use only trusted Wi-Fi networks; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi.
* Regularly review privacy settings on apps and social media accounts.
* Log out from shared or public devices after use.

**Report Cybercrime:**

* National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
* Helpline Number: 1930
